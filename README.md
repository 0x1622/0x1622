## About Me

I'm `0x1622`, a Computer Science student at BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus.

Most of my work sits around reverse engineering, malware analysis, digital forensics, and CTF problem solving. I like breaking down binaries, studying Android malware behavior, writing up the interesting parts, and building small tools that make analysis workflows cleaner.

## Current Focus

- Reverse engineering workflows for native and Android targets.
- Malware and RAT analysis, especially behavior, obfuscation, and unpacking paths.
- CTFs with Team L3ak across reverse, crypto, and systems-oriented challenges.
- Going deeper into binary exploitation through hands-on practice and pwn.college.
